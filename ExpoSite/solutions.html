What can be done?

The problem is difficult to address, especially without considering the numerous affected groups. There are narrow solutions, we need wide ones which involve these groups.

Industry-wide bias recognition and checking:

- This is a wide approach that could easily be pushed under the rug without a technical component like the next one.

- This, however, would be a great move by the industry leaders to recognize their results can be flawed and to promote individuals do their own research on the topic to become educated.

Search Engine Bias recognition tool:

- This is true technical tool that can be added to search engine results that could promote transparency for the users of the search engine.

- See the paper for more information on the tool: Search Engine Gender Bias.” Frontiers in big data vol. 4 622106. 26 May. 2021, doi:10.3389/fdata.2021.622106

Push for development changes:

- No political or commercial backing – unlikely in the Attention Economy (see definitions)

- Not optimized for the user (see definition for Search Engine Optimization) – unlikely because of the way the internet has progressed into individualization

Request results that are not reliant on aspects of one’s Identity:

- Difficult to get right because the journal did not quite hit all of the affected identities.

- A future solution that can be pursued with a more tangible fix.

Modifying the algorithm to promote diversity and respect in results

- Done in this study:M. Mehrab Tanjim et al., "Generating and Controlling Diversity in Image Search," 2022 IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV), 2022, pp. 3908-3916, doi: 10.1109/WACV51458.2022.00396.
